Who We Are

At Mile High Aerials, we are a team of Part 107 certified remote drone pilots specializing in artial photography, cinematography, photogrammetry, and first person view (FPV) fly-throughs. Fueled by a passion for capturing breathtaking and unique moments from above, we provide customer-focused drone services tailed to our clients' needs.
